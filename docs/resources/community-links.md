## Community Guidelines

### Be Respectful

✅ **Be civil** — Treat others with respect  
✅ **Be constructive** — Provide helpful feedback  
✅ **Be honest** — Share honest opinions  
✅ **Be supportive** — Support other community members  

### Don't

❌ **Don't spam** — Avoid spam and repetition  
❌ **Don't promote scams** — Don't promote fake projects  
❌ **Don't share keys** — Never share private keys  
❌ **Don't harass** — Don't harass other members  

## Community Participation

### How to Participate

1. **Join community channels** — Join Discord, Telegram, etc.
2. **Introduce yourself** — Say hello to the community
3. **Ask questions** — Ask questions in appropriate channels
4. **Share feedback** — Share constructive feedback
5. **Help others** — Help other community members

### Community Roles

- **Community Members** — Regular participants
- **Moderators** — Help manage community
- **Ambassadors** — Represent community
- **Contributors** — Contribute to project
